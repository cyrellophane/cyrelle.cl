Discovery Channel Shark Week

Shark Week is Discovery Channel's biggest event of the year, and in the summer of 2016 the Discovery Channel Store developed its biggest marketing event in the store's history.

Working closely with our copywriter, photographer, marketing team, and coding team, I designed a series of content pages with supporting marketing assets.

From concept to completion, I partnered with the copywriter to spearhead the the project, and delivered on tight deadlines.

Discovery Channel 2016 Shark Week was one of the most complex projects I worked on while I was a designer at Delivery Agent. It centered around four conceptual content pages with various supporting marketing pieces for different platforms and placements.

There were also numerous connected promotional marketing pushes. In the end over 80 different pieces were created.
